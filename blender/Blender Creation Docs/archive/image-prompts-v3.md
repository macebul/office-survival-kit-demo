## Usage Notes

- All flat panel prompts are designed for use with Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion
- For the hero product photo, add **"--ar 4:3"** (Midjourney) or equivalent aspect ratio for a standard product shot
- For flat panel artboard prompts, use the following aspect ratios:
  - Artboard 1 (Lid / Top Surface, 14" × 11.5"): **"--ar 14:11.5"** or approximately **"--ar 5:4"**
  - Artboard 2 (Front Face, 14" × 5"): **"--ar 14:5"** or approximately **"--ar 3:1"**
  - Artboard 3 (Back Face, 14" × 5"): **"--ar 14:5"** or approximately **"--ar 3:1"**
  - Artboard 4 (Bottom Surface, 14" × 11.5"): **"--ar 14:11.5"** or approximately **"--ar 5:4"**
  - Artboards 5 & 6 (Edges, 11.5" × 5"): **"--ar 11.5:5"** or approximately **"--ar 7:3"**
- These are directional prompts — outputs will need refinement. The Nutrition Facts panel in particular may require multiple attempts to get the label format reading correctly
- The Margaritaville sticker on the bottom surface may require a separate standalone image generation pass and compositing into the panel
- The booking frame in Interior Shot 1 is a SEPARATE OBJECT resting in the case — do NOT generate it as a hole in the lid
