### The Change

The interior has been redesigned from a vertical 3-tray stack to a **2-zone side-by-side layout with a hidden mug compartment.**

**Front zone (facing the person):** Trays 1 and 2 stacked vertically with coil-bound booklets between them. Trays use **die-cut recesses (Pelican case style)** — molded cutouts for each item with chalk-outline silhouettes and item name + tagline printed on the tray surface. No ring clearance needed; trays are centered in the front zone.

**Back zone (along back wall):** A hidden padded compartment containing the Mug Shot mug (on its side, handle to the side), Corporate Parole Passport and Yard Pass flanking left and right. Completely hidden while trays are in place. Revealed when Tray 2 is lifted and a lift-off cover is removed.

**There is no Tray 3.** The mug compartment replaces it. There are now **two booklets** (not three) — one on each tray.
