## Narrative Context (NEW in v6)

The kit has two fictional characters whose voices appear throughout:

- **The OG** — the original creator of the Office Survival Kit. A veteran office survivor who built the kit as an underground guide. His voice is dry, deadpan, and institutional. He appears on sticky notes printed on item boxes, in the activity guide, and on hidden surfaces throughout the kit.
- **The Corporate Nemesis** — a sleazy, self-unaware marketing executive who found the OG's kit, mass-produced it, and is selling it on Amazon. His voice is corporate buzzwords, fake enthusiasm, and trademark symbols on nothing. He appears on the retail item box packaging, the outer Amazon box, and the product guide.

**Why items are individually boxed:** The nemesis has repackaged each item as a retail product. The boxes are part of the joke — they look like real products you'd find on a shelf, complete with over-the-top marketing copy. The OG fights back by leaving sticky notes on/in the boxes with his real commentary.
