## ARTBOARD 5 — Left Edge (11.5" × 5.0")

### Element 18: MONDAY
**Treatment:** Ransom note style — each letter of MONDAY is cut from a different newspaper/magazine source. Different font, size, weight, and color per letter. Large, arranged at a diagonal angle across the surface. The word is MONDAY and nothing else. No icons — the typography IS the design.

> **MONDAY**

---

## ARTBOARD 6 — Right Edge (11.5" × 5.0")

### Element 19: Oval Travel Sticker Cluster
**Treatment:** 2–3 oval destination stickers, slightly overlapping, clustered together. Each is a different color. Looks collected over time, not applied all at once. Pairs with Margaritaville on the bottom to reinforce the well-traveled luggage narrative.

**Sticker 1:**
> CFR3
> Conference Room 3

**Sticker 2:**
> BRK2
> Break Room

**Sticker 3:**
> PKG-B
> Parking Lot B
